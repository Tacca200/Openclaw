Privacy Policy

Openclaw is a personal tool for uploading creator-provided videos to TikTok as drafts.

This application may process basic TikTok account data required for authentication and content upload.

This application does not sell personal data.

Uploaded content and tokens are used only to enable TikTok draft upload functionality.

Data is retained only as needed to operate the application.

Users can revoke TikTok access at any time through TikTok account settings.
